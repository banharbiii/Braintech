About BrainCo

BrainCo is a neurotechnology company developing cutting-edge brainwave monitoring solutions to improve mental performance, focus, and wellbeing. With roots at Harvard University, the company has built groundbreaking wearable technologies that enhance attention, mental fitness, and health. BrainCo combines neuroscience, AI, and hardware engineering to deliver real-world applications of brain-computer interface technologies across healthcare, education, and lifestyle sectors.

Dr. Bicheng Han, the founder of BrainCo, holds a PhD in biomedical engineering and has a passion for combining neuroscience with AI. Through his leadership, BrainCo emerged from the Harvard Innovation Lab to become a pioneer in wearable brainwave technology. His work has paved the way for accessible neurofeedback solutions that help people improve mental focus, reduce stress, and enhance cognitive ability.

Transforming Lives with Technology

BrainCo is redefining what’s possible in human-machine interaction. Its flagship product FocusCalm helps individuals reduce stress through brain training. The company also innovates in assistive technology, offering brain-controlled prosthetics that restore independence to amputees. BrainCo's educational neurofeedback systems are used in schools to boost student performance by training focus and attention. All of these solutions reflect BrainCo’s mission to unlock the brain’s full potential using science-backed tools.
